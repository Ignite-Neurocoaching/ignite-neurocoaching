Common questions

How do I know which program is right for me?
Book a free discovery call and we'll figure it out together. Every client is different — the right program depends on your goals, timeline, and how you work best.
Do sessions take place online or in person?
All sessions are held online via Zoom, making it accessible from anywhere in the world with a stable connection.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients notice shifts within the first few sessions. Lasting neurological change typically takes consistent work over 8–12 weeks — which is exactly what these programs are designed to deliver.
Is this therapy or coaching?
Neurocoaching is not therapy. We work forward-focused on goals, patterns, and performance — not diagnosis or treatment of mental health conditions. If you're unsure, the discovery call is a great place to ask.
What if I need to reschedule a session?
Life happens. Sessions can be rescheduled with 24 hours notice. Flexibility is built into every program to accommodate real life.
Are payment plans available?
Yes — all programs offer flexible payment options. We'll discuss what works for you on the discovery call.
